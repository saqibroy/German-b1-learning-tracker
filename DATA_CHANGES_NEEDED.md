**Definition:**
```
CRITICAL telc B1 Format: You write only ONE email/letter (not three tasks!) responding 
to 4 specific Leitpunkte (guiding points) in exactly 30 minutes. Target: 120-150 words.

Typical Scenarios:
- Semi-formal: Apartment manager, company inquiry, course registration, complaint
- Informal: Friend's invitation, giving advice, sharing plans

The 4 Leitpunkte Structure:
The exam gives you a situation and 4 points you MUST address:
1. Often a question (e.g., "When can you come?")
2. Request for information (e.g., "Ask about the price")
3. Give your opinion (e.g., "What do you think about...?")
4. Suggest/propose something (e.g., "Make a suggestion for...")

YOU MUST ADDRESS ALL 4 POINTS CLEARLY!
```

Example Task (Informal):

**Tips:**
```
telc B1 Writing Strategy:
1. READ CAREFULLY - Identify all 4 Leitpunkte and underline them
2. PLAN (3 min) - Quick notes for each point
3. WRITE (20 min) - Address points in order, use connecting words
4. CHECK (7 min) - Did you answer ALL 4 points? Verb position? Articles?

Time Management is KEY: Practice in exactly 30 minutes (not 60 like other exams!)

Common Mistakes to Avoid:
- Missing one of the 4 points (instant point deduction!)
- Too short (<100 words) or too long (>180 words)
- Wrong formality level (use 'du' for friends, 'Sie' for formal)
- Forgetting greeting/closing
```
